## Interpretation

The search can reproduce the pair table exactly only through a
lookup-like bucketization. The best all-row candidate uses more
than the compact bucket limit and does not beat shuffled-label
controls as a meaningful generator.

The compact candidates are the relevant formula test. Their best
hit count and MDL gain do not promote an algebraic generator.
