The best denominator is not evidence of hidden lattice structure:
its cutpoint hits are below the same-shape random-control mean,
and no prefix-selected denominator covers held-out books or beats
the held-out random p95.

## Decision

- No cutpoint-lattice generator is promoted.
- Small proportional grids are rejected as an operation-skeleton source.
- This closes a natural source-free boundary hypothesis without changing the formula.
- This does not change the compression bound.
- Row0 remains exogenous and unchanged.
- No plaintext, translation, semantic reading, or case reopening is introduced.
